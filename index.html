Aska Generator¶

This is a documentation for Aska Generator.

What’s Aska?¶

Aska is an opinion-sharing platform that can be used for open-ended surveys, brainstorming, etc. You, as a host, propose topics (questions) and ask users to post their responses.

Basic usage¶

To use Aska, you build an Aska instance using Aska generator. You can check the results and progress of your Aska instance at Dashboard.

You can generate a demo instance for free. To activate a full version of Aska, Aska project team will manually upgrade your demo instance.

Composition¶

Aska is mainly composed of Aska generator, Aska instance, and Dashboard. Aska generator and Dashboard should be accessible to hosts only.

type-A and type-B Aska¶

Aska has two basic modes called type-A and type-B. These are more fundamental than other modes (e.g., the hashtag, sign-in, and readonly modes). If your instance version is higher than DEMO version, you can choose type-A or type-B, in addition to the choices of other modes.

Please find Question types for details.
